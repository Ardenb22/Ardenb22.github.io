Divergent by Veronica Roth

A world divided into five factions: Dauntless the brave and the reckless, Amity the peaceful, Candor the honest, Erudite the smart, and Abnegation the selfless. You have to fit onto one thing but what happens when you turn sixteen and don't fit into any one faction? You are divergent. Divergent means you have equal aptitude for multiple factions. Tris the main character is Divergent she has equal aptitude for Erudite, Dauntless, and abnegation. But what faction will she choose to go to? And who will help Tris navigate the tricky reality of being Divergent?
